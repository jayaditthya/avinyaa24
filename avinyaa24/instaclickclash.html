About Insta Click Clash


Description :

Capture the world through your lens in the photography competition. Unleash your creativity, showcase unique perspectives, and tell compelling visual stories. Join us in celebrating the art of toggling between moments, emotions, and landscapes. Let your photos speak louder than words and illuminate the extraordinary in the ordinary.

Rules :

This event includes only one participant.

This is an online event and the winner will be requested to come to the college to collect the cash prize on 5th of February.

Participants are allowed to post their pictures in the google form link that is embeded in the form of QR code in the poster.

Participants are allowed to edit their pictures by using any AI or editing application.

Partcipants are allowed to post only one picture in the google form. Posting multiple pictures may lead to disqualification.

The size of the image should not exceed 10MB.


Evaluation :

We will post your uploaded photo in our instagram handle.

The participant’s photo which has got more number of likes will be the winner.

Winner will be announced on 4th of February in our official Instagram handle.
